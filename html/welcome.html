Welcome to WordSave!

WordSave is a free pocket dictionary for people wanting to expand their vocabulary while also learning correct pronounciation.

How to Use

WordSave relies on keyboard shortcuts to make saving and viewing words easier:

  1. Select a word and save using Control + Shift + S
  2. View the WordSave dashboard with Control + Shift + L

For full interactive functionality the cursor tracker has been automatically enabled. It can be disabled in the settings.
